  2. Mix_Resume

停止

  1. Mix_HaltChannel
  2. Mix_ExpireChannel
  3. Mix_FadeOutChannel
  4. Mix_ChannelFinished

  2. Mix_Paused
  3. Mix_FadingChannel
  4. Mix_GetChunk
